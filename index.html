About

Hi, I'm Jian Genovaña

I'm a Digital Designer based in Makati City, Philippines, who's passionate about transforming digital experiences into visual languages and connecting ideas to customer perception with a little bit of magic, fairy dust and pixels.

Hello!

With 7+ years in the game, I've worked on all kinds of projects, bringing ideas to life and nailing every digital detail. I'm known for being adaptable, ambitious, and super chill, with a big creative spark—I seriously live and breathe design.

Right now, I’m vibing at Canva under nXscale as a Digital Designer, where I'm all about designing, producing, and managing digital assets and thumbnails across the site. I also get to mix and match pre-selected components to create unique designs that hit hard on websites and social media. Before this, I worked with an offshore gaming company and ad agencies, cranking out promo banners, motion graphics, and web designs for landing pages. I even designed ads for pitchside boards at football matches—talk about flexing that creative muscle!
