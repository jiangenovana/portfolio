About

Hi, I'm Jian Genovaña

I'm a Digital Designer based in Makati City, Philippines, who's passionate about transforming digital experiences into visual languages and connecting ideas to customer perception with a little bit of magic, fairy dust and pixels.

Hello!

With over 8 years in the creative field, I’ve built a solid career crafting impactful visuals that blend creativity, strategy, and storytelling. From motion graphics and brand campaigns to social media content and print design, I’ve done it all—and I love every second of it.

Right now, I’m designing and managing all things visual for Ferguson Education and Migration, where I handle everything from social media strategies to local and international marketing materials. Before that, I was with Canva under nXscale as a Digital Designer, creating digital assets and thumbnails that help elevate the user experience across the platform.

I’ve also collaborated with brands like 321 Films and Conex Media as a freelance designer, producing motion graphics and social content that drive engagement. My earlier experience in the offshore gaming and advertising industries honed my eye for detail and fast-paced adaptability—plus, I even had my work displayed on LED boards during European football matches.

Adaptable, ambitious, and creatively driven—I’m all about turning ideas into visuals that don’t just look good, but connect.
